98 MB · Apple Silicon · macOS 12 (Monterey) or newer

Install

  1. Open the downloaded .dmg.
  2. Drag Rip Raptor into your Applications folder.
  3. First launch only: right-click the app and pick Open, then click Open in the dialog. macOS shows a warning for ad-hoc-signed apps the first time; once you click through, double-click works as normal.
  4. Drop URLs in, hit Rip It!

What it does

  • Downloads from hundreds of sites via yt-dlp.
  • Pick quality (Best, 2160p / 1440p / 1080p / 720p / 480p / 360p, audio-only).
  • Embeds subtitles and chapters when available.
  • Built-in trim, crop, and concat editor.
  • Send-to-Rip-Raptor bookmarklet for one-click capture from any tab.
  • Persistent searchable history.

Requirements

  • macOS 12 or newer.
  • Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3/M4) — Intel Macs not supported in this beta.

What's bundled

  • yt-dlp 2026.03.17
  • ffmpeg 8.1 (arm64 static)
  • ffprobe 8.1

No Homebrew or pipx required.
